Hi, I'm Elijah! A little about me;

  • My philosophy in life is centered around the principle of self-improvement. I constantly strive to overcome new challenges while learning new skills along the way. As a result, I have accumulated a plethora of skill sets.
  • I am an auto-didact (e.g., a self learner). I love to solve problems, fix broken things, and find better and more efficient ways of doing things. I am also what you call a "do-it-yourself" enthusiast.
  • My current passions and interests include software programming, music composition, audio and video recording, music and video production, live production, field recording, and motorcycle riding.
  • For fun, I love to ride my Kawasaki Ninja 400, socialize with friends and family, visit Olympia National Park, host games nights featuring table-top board games, build or fix computers, tinker with new technology, watch movies, and play PC games or the Nintendo Switch.
  • My most recent jobs involved Client Services Coordination and Program Coordination in Healthcare Administration. In the past, I was a telecommunications technician in the Army. Currently, I am looking to re-enter the field of Information Technology.
